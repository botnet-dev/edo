Our Impact

PROJECT 1
Construction of the Community Development/Training Center

Construction of The Community Center in Ifakara, aiming to improve the living conditions for youth, young mothers, their children, orphaned girls, survivors of sexual and gender-based violence, and young women in precarious situations or particularly difficult socioeconomic circumstances.

PROJECT 2
YHTS24-Ifakara

1st July 2024 to 30th June 2025
75 young mothers from 9 wards in Ifakara Town Council were selected to attend Employability Skills in Tailoring and Horticulture.

PROJECT 3
Community Sensitization and mobilization in Malaria Intervention

July 2023 and ended February 2024
Supported by Global Fund through National Malaria Control Program (NMCP)Ministry of Health. It was implemented in Ulanga and Morogoro District Councils.
