Our Values

Mission

Building a nation of highly educated/skilled girls and children, who withstand challenges, who are economically independent with excellent services to the community, who respect human rights and enhancing gender equality.

Vision

Our vision is to anticipate having youth, girls, and children with a positive outlook on the attainment of sustainable development.

Goals

  • Raise awareness for youth and girls on their rights.
  • Promote community engagement in formulating by-laws for accountability.
  • Encourage modern agriculture and environmental conservation.
  • Empower vulnerable children with education, health, and housing.
