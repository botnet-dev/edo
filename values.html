Our Values

Mission

Building a nation of highly educated/skilled girls and children, who withstand challenges, who are economically independent with excellent services to the community, who respect human rights and enhancing gender equality, with good stewardship in managing and using available resources.

Vision

To anticipate having youth, girls and children with a positive outlook on the attainment of sustainable development.

Objectives

  • Raising awareness to youth and girls on their rights to engage in different social, political and economic activities.
  • To improve healthcare systems in remote and underserved areas.
  • To ensure clean and sustainable water solutions for communities in need.
  • To promote community-driven development projects that foster self-sustainability.
  • To advocate for environmental conservation and sustainable living.
