About The EDO

We are dedicated to improving the lives of underprivileged communities through sustainable projects focusing on education, justice, and fighting against poverty. Founded in 2018, we have grown to serve hundreds of people in Morogoro region in Tanzania.

Our Strategic Plan

Teamwork

Building a nation of highly educated/skilled girls and children, who withstand challenges, are economically independent, and provide excellent services to the community, respecting human rights and enhancing gender equality with good stewardship of available resources.

Vision

To anticipate a future where youth, girls, and children have a positive outlook on achieving sustainable development.

Purpose

EDO enhances employment opportunities for youth, young women, and young mothers through vocational skills development, agriculture, and horticulture training.
