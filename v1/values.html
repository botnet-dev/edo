Our Values

Mission

Building a nation of highly educated/skilled girls and children, who withstand challenges, are economically independent, and provide excellent services to the community, respecting human rights and enhancing gender equality with good stewardship of available resources.

Vision

To anticipate a future where youth, girls, and children have a positive outlook on achieving sustainable development.

Objectives

  • Raise awareness among youth and girls on their rights to engage in social, political, and economic activities.
  • Involve community members in formulating by-laws to ensure accountability and responsibility in community activities, such as attending village meetings.
  • Empower women and other gender groups to participate in activities that help mitigate and adapt to the impacts of climate change.
  • Promote community-driven development projects that foster self-sustainability.
  • Advocate for environmental conservation and sustainable living.
