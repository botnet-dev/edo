The EDO and Values

EDO is a community development organization aimed at enhancing employment opportunities for youth, young women, and mothers by providing vocational skills, education, and support. Established in 2018 and officially registered in 2021, EDO fights against poverty, injustice, and gender inequality.

At The EDO, we believe, work and abide the following core values, which we believe are the foundations of our endeavours.

Our Core Values

  Teamwork

Our team is supportive of each other and loyal to one another.

  Good governance

We shall adopt the best possible process for making and implementing decisions.

  Respect

We treat our team members and all stakeholders with mutual respect.

  Accountability

Our team is supportive of each other and loyal to one another.

  Transparency

We shall be bound by transparency in our work.
