About The EDO

We are dedicated to improving the lives of underprivileged communities through sustainable projects focusing on education, healthcare, and access to clean water. Founded in 2000, we have grown to serve thousands of people worldwide.

A Glance about Us

The Enlighten Development Organization (EDO) is a community development organization established to fight poverty and injustice by promoting education and equality.

Our Purpose

EDO enhances employment opportunities for youth, young women, and young mothers through vocational skills development, agriculture, and horticulture training.

Our Mission

Building a nation of highly educated/skilled girls and children, who withstand challenges and are economically independent.
