EDO aims to enhance the employability skills/opportunities for marginalized women, children, youth and young mothers through improving vocational skills development (VSD) on horticultural training, tailoring training and entrepreneurship training, food additional value chain and life skills education.

All these training activities are conducted at the EDO Community Center and spanning between three months to six months per batch.
