Construction of community development center

Ended in 2021

This project aims to train 75 young mothers in tailoring and horticulture to improve their employability. The program includes theory, practical training, and post-training support.

Project Objectives

  • Improve the living conditions for youth, young mothers, orphaned girls, survivors of sexual and gender-based violence, and young women in challenging socioeconomic situations.
  • Being training and social retreat, especially for young mothers and women who are often rejected, traumatized, and socially marginalized.

Empower Young Mothers through Horticulture and Tailoring Skills

Started: July 1, 2024 - Ends: June 30, 2025

This project aims to train 75 young mothers in tailoring and horticulture to improve their employability. The program includes theory, practical training, and post-training support.

Project Objectives

  • Provide employability skills in tailoring and horticulture.
  • Offer hands-on experience with practical training sessions.
  • Support beneficiaries with startup kits and coaching for business establishment.

Community Sensitization in Malaria Intervention

Duration: July 2023 - February 2024

This project, supported by the Global Fund, aimed to mobilize communities for malaria prevention in the Ulanga and Morogoro districts.

Project Objectives

  • Increase community awareness on malaria prevention methods.
  • Encourage community participation in health interventions.
  • Support local health services in combating malaria.
