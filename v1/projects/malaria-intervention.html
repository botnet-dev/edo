The EDO Projects

Community Sensitization and Mobilization in Malaria Intervention

> Reducing Malaria through Community Engagement in Ulanga and Morogoro

Project Overview

The Community Sensitization and Mobilization in Malaria Intervention Project was launched to increase awareness and mobilize communities in the fight against malaria. This project was supported by the Global Fund through the National Malaria Control Program (NMCP) under the Ministry of Health.

Timeline and Location

Running from July 2023 to February 2024, the project was implemented in the Ulanga and Morogoro District Councils, targeting high-risk communities to improve malaria prevention and intervention practices.

Project Support and Impact

Supported by Global Fund resources, this project focused on community-driven engagement to enhance malaria prevention efforts in these regions. Through education and mobilization, the initiative aimed to reduce malaria transmission rates and promote healthier communities.
